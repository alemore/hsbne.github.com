Welcome!

HSBNE, the HackerSpace Brisbane, is a community workshop located in Sumner

We're a group of people brought together by a shared interest in tinkering, creating, and making things. HSBNE is entirely run by its members — we have no employees or staff. Everything that happens here is thanks to the shared effort of our community.

Our space currently includes:

  • Woodworking
  • Digital Fabrication
  • Metalworking
  • Electronics
  • Crafts
The Shop

After more than 18 months in storage, we finally set up shop in Sumner around March 2024, where we share the premises with Accelerando Consulting. Since then, members have spent countless hours getting the space ready for projects and tinkering. Everything that happens at HSBNE exists thanks to the dedication and hard work of our members.

Mitre Station

The mitre station is part of our woodworking area and also includes other useful machines like a sharpening setup and a drill press.

CNC Router

Our Blue Elephant CNC router features an automatic tool changer and a vacuum bed, making it easy to cut sheet materials and natural wood with precision. Members also have access to VCarve Makerspace Edition for designing and preparing toolpaths.

Machining and Laser Cutting

We’re setting up a shared area in this room for machining and laser cutting. Our CO₂ laser will be upgraded before the end of the year, and we plan to add a milling machine soon.

3D Printing

Our 3D printing area is powered by a Bambu X1 Carbon and a dedicated slicing computer, making high-quality printing easy and approachable for beginners. Just load your model, slice, and start your print in minutes.

Electronics & 3D Printing

This shared workspace combines our 3D printing and electronics areas. Members can prototype, test, and refine their ideas using tools like our Bambu X1 Carbon printer, soldering stations, oscilloscopes, and dedicated computers for design and slicing.

Electronics Bench

Our electronics bench is set up for soldering, testing, and small repairs. It includes power supplies, microscopes, and oscilloscopes to help you work on a wide range of electronic projects.

Open Days

Visit during the day and explore what we do

Every Saturday from 10 am – 2 pm, our doors are open to the public. Come for a tour at 11 am, stay for a chat, and enjoy pizza around 12:30 pm. It’s the perfect time to see the space in action and meet our members.

Open Nights

Drop in after hours and see the space come alive

Every Tuesday evening, HSBNE opens its doors for the public to explore, chat, and create. Members start arriving from 5 pm, and a guided tour of the workshop begins at 6:30 pm. Whether you’re curious or already building something — come hang out!
